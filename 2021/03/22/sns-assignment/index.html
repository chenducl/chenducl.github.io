Introduction

What is the problem you are trying to solve and the general framework to solve it. 1 to 2 pages

The COVID-19 pandemic, one of the deadliest pandemics in history, was first identified in December 2019, and has influenced more than 180 countries in the world. Aiming at establishing effective policies to reduce the pandemic spread, massive efforts have been carried out by governments and researchers for the best synergy. Specifically, the capabilities of analyzing the transmission patterns and forecasting the trends of the pandemic allow the goverments to perform proper actions to inhibit its spread efficiently. Numerous time series methods have been proposed to forecast the COVID-19 cases[1], epidemiological trends[2], hospitalizations[3], vaccine efficacy trials[4], etc.

In this assignment, we focus on forecasting COVID-19 cases and Google Trends. To obtain a comprehensive understanding of the pandemic trends, we collect diverse data sources, including the global cases, mobility, hospitilizations, medical care and vaccinations data.

The use of neural networks enable us to develop a model that learns the inherent patterns in these past observations and then predict the future values with the trained model. Specifically, we implement long short-term memory (LSTM)[4:1] and transformer[5] models with different structures and hyperparameters settings for time series. To select the best fitting model from model candidates, various experiments have been conducted on deciding the best model structures and hyperparameters in our forcasting task.
